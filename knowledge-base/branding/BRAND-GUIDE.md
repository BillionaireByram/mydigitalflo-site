# My Digital Flo — Brand Guide

## Brand Identity

**Company Name:** My Digital Flo (also: Digital Flo, MyDigitalFlo)

**Tagline:** AI Infrastructure for Autonomous People

**Core Message:** Stop Renting Intelligence. Own It.

---

## Brand Positioning

**What We Are:**
AI infrastructure company that builds done-for-you AI systems for businesses.

**What We're NOT:**
- Not another SaaS tool
- Not a chatbot company
- Not an agency selling retainers

**The Ownership Model:**
We build AI systems that clients OWN — not rent. One investment, permanent ownership. No monthly AI subscriptions.

## Typography

**Primary Font:** Inter
- Headlines: 700-800 weight
- Body: 400-500 weight
- UI Elements: 500-600 weight

**Style:**
- Clean, modern, tech-forward
- Sans-serif throughout
- Generous letter-spacing on eyebrows/labels

---

## Visual Style

**Overall Aesthetic:**
- Dark mode by default
- Futuristic but approachable
- Premium, high-end tech feel
- Apple-meets-Blade-Runner vibe

**Key Visual Elements:**
- Glassmorphism cards
- Subtle gradients
- Glowing accents on hover
- Neural network / connected node motifs
- Grid patterns (subtle)

**Imagery:**
- Abstract tech visualizations
- AI/neural network graphics
- Dark backgrounds with blue accents
- No stock photos of people smiling at laptops

---

## Logo

**Primary Logo:** Digital Flo wordmark with wave/flow icon
- Electric blue icon

**Usage:**
- Always on dark backgrounds
- Minimum clear space around logo
- Don't stretch, rotate, or alter colors

---

## Voice & Tone

**Brand Voice:**
- Confident but not arrogant
- Direct and clear
- Slightly provocative (challenges status quo)
- Tech-savvy but accessible
- No corporate jargon

**Tone Guidelines:**
- Lead with value, not features
- Use "you/your" language
- Ask questions that make people think
- Create contrast (renting vs owning)

**Words We Use:**
- Own, ownership, yours
- Intelligence, system, infrastructure
- Build, integrate, centralize
- Freedom, control, autonomy

**Words We Avoid:**
- "AI-powered" (overused)
- "Revolutionary" (cliché)
- "Synergy" (corporate speak)
- "Cutting-edge" (vague)

---

## Brand Applications

**Website:** Dark theme, electric blue accents, glassmorphism cards
**Social Media:** Same visual language, punchy copy
**Presentations:** Dark slides, minimal text, high impact
**Documents:** Clean, branded headers, consistent typography
