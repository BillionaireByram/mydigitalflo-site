**Type:** Done-For-You AI Infrastructure Build

## The Problem We Solve

### The AI Trap
Business owners are:
- Paying $300-500/month for fragmented AI tools
- Duct-taping 15+ different subscriptions together
- Being the "integration layer" themselves
- Renting intelligence they'll never own

### Pain Points
1. **Subscription Bleeding:** $6K+/year on tools that don't connect
2. **Time Drain:** 10-20 hours/week on "glue work"
3. **No Ownership:** Subject to price hikes, feature changes, shutdowns
4. **Complexity:** Managing multiple logins, dashboards, workflows

Instead of renting scattered AI tools, clients get ONE centralized system they OWN.

1. **Custom AI System** — Built for their specific business, workflows, voice
2. **Full Integration** — Connected to CRM, calendar, email, communications
3. **Voice + Text Agents** — 24/7 automated responses (AVA)
4. **Smart Qualification** — AI filters leads, books only qualified calls
5. **Performance Dashboard** — Real-time metrics and insights
6. **Complete Ownership** — No monthly AI subscriptions, no vendor lock-in
